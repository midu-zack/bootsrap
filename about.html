ABOUT US

GRID SMARTS

SISCO products provide real-time communications and integration capabilities for applications in the energy utility industry. OEMs, system integrators, and end users use our products throughout the world to cost-effectively build solutions supporting international open standards.

SISCO is an employee-owned company founded in 1983 that is dedicated to applying standards to address real-world problems. We offer real-time communications and application integration products and services that are used worldwide by leading OEMs, system integrators, and end users. We connect customers to information by providing integrated solutions based on open internationally accepted standards. Our mission to create value is served by our skilled and motivated employees that are dedicated to the success of our customers.

MISSION

Helping customers create value with open interoperable standards by providing solutions based on knowledge, experience, and innovation.

VISION

Transforming the world of energy using open standards.

VALUES

Trust: We build trust with our employees, partners, and customers by upholding our values, promoting openness, and following through on our commitments.
Quality: We value the best people, best practices, resilient solutions, and continuous improvement.
Collaboratio: We believe that shared goals and mutual support lead to greater success.
Innovation: We understand problems and solve them with unique solutions.
